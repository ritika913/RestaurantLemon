About Little Lemon

Little Lemon is more than a restaurant — it’s a celebration of flavors, freshness, and family. Founded in 2020, we’ve been bringing people together over authentic Mediterranean and Italian-inspired dishes. Our philosophy is simple: fresh ingredients, bold taste, and a cozy atmosphere that feels like home.

Our Story

Founded by two brothers with a shared passion for great food, Little Lemon started as a small family café. Over the years, we’ve grown into a full-service restaurant known for our signature lemon-based dishes, handcrafted sauces, and warm hospitality.

Our Team

Our talented chefs and friendly staff work hand-in-hand to craft memorable dining experiences. Each dish is prepared with creativity, care, and a touch of lemon zest — symbolizing our belief in freshness and joy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a space where good food meets good company. Whether you’re dining in, reserving for a family event, or ordering to-go — we promise to serve happiness in every bite.
